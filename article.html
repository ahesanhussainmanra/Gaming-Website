Crosshair Discipline That Wins More First Fights

Most missed duels come from delayed crosshair setup, not weak aim mechanics. This playbook fixes that with repeatable habits.

1. Pre-Aim Before Contact

Start aiming at likely enemy height before peeking. This reduces panic flicks and improves first-shot control.

2. Clear Angles In Sequence

Do not swing wide without a clear order. Slice one angle at a time and stabilize movement before shooting.

3. Reset After Every Engagement

After each duel, recenter your crosshair and breathing. Small resets stop mechanical breakdown during long rounds.

4. Track Progress Weekly

Measure opening duel success and first-bullet accuracy. If both metrics improve, your routine is working.
