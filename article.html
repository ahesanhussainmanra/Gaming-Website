Best Gaming Mouse Under Rs 500

How to choose a budget mouse that still delivers stable tracking and reliable clicks.

Quick Buying Strategy

At this price point, ignore flashy packaging and focus on shape comfort, sensor stability and switch feel. If your grip is claw or fingertip, choose compact shells around 60-75 grams. For palm grip, prefer medium shell size with wider hump support.

Checklist Before Buying

  • Sensor should track consistently at your regular DPI range.
  • Left and right click should feel similar in pressure.
  • Cable should be soft enough to avoid drag on quick flicks.
  • Feet should glide smoothly and be replaceable if possible.

Recommended Flow

First, shortlist two options based on hand size. Next, compare real user feedback on double-click issues and build quality. Finally, test with your in-game sens for 2-3 days before final judgment.

Final Verdict

If you prioritize sensor consistency and shape fit, even a low-budget mouse can deliver strong ranked performance. Avoid overpaying for RGB and focus on control.
